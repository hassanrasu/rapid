Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the Rapid Companies ecosystem.

Rapid Companies is a family of eight specialized construction and real estate businesses based in Orlando, Florida. Each company handles a different stage of the build, from land acquisition and design to steel erection, roofing, interior fit-out, and facility operations. Founded by Richie Webster, the group has delivered over 16 million square feet of self-storage construction across the U.S.

Not always. Each company has its own specialty. On large projects, several companies may collaborate across the full pipeline. On smaller jobs, you might work with just one, like Rapid Roofing for a re-roof or Rapid Doors & Hallways for an interior fit-out.

Headquartered in Orlando, Florida at 2345 W. Sand Lake Rd., Suite 100, Orlando, FL 32809. The companies hold GC licenses in multiple states across the East Coast and have completed projects in Florida, the Carolinas, Tennessee, and beyond.

You can hire any single company on its own. Need just roofing? Call Rapid Roofing. Want a full turn-key build from dirt to doors? The family coordinates together on one project.

The Rapid family has over 30 years of combined experience in self-storage construction. Rapid Building Solutions was founded in 2016 and scaled quickly within a few years. Several sister companies were established in 2020 as the group expanded.

Vertical integration. Most projects require hiring separate architects, engineers, general contractors, roofers, and interior specialists from different firms. With Rapid, every handoff stays in the family. Fewer miscommunications, faster timelines, lower costs.

The construction companies focus on commercial work, primarily self-storage facilities and steel buildings. Your Storage Units serves individual consumers who need storage space, but Rapid does not do residential homebuilding.
